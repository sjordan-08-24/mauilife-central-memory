### The Team Hub Application

The Team Hub is where every team member starts their day. It replaces scattered task lists, Slack searches, and "where do I find..." questions with a single, intelligent command center.

**Smart Task Queue**

All work flows into one prioritized queue:
- Tasks auto-assigned based on skills, location, and workload
- Priority calculated from SLA deadlines, guest impact, and urgency
- Real-time rebalancing when priorities change
- Clear visibility into what's next and what's waiting

**Task Queue Features:**
- Filter by property, task type, priority, or assignee
- Bulk actions for similar tasks
- Quick-complete for routine items
- Escalation triggers for overdue items
- Notes and photo attachment on any task

**Route Optimization**

For field team members, the day's tasks are organized by optimal driving route:
- Minimizes drive time between properties
- Accounts for task duration estimates
- Real-time rerouting when priorities change
- Integration with maps for navigation
- Estimated completion time for the full day

**Performance Dashboard**

Every team member sees their personal metrics:
- Quality scores from inspection reviews
- On-time completion percentage

**Team Calendar**

**Notification Center**

Priority-ranked alerts filtered by relevance:

**Seamless Chat Interface**

### The Guest Journey

The guest relationship follows a carefully designed journey from booking through rebooking:

**Phase 1: Booking Confirmed**

When a guest booksâ€”whether 2 weeks or 12 months out:
- Welcome message sent immediately
- Property details and what to expect
- Pre-arrival questionnaire link
- We begin learning about their trip

**Phase 2: Learning Phase**

Between booking and arrival, we gather information:
- Who's traveling (adults, children, ages)
- Purpose of trip (anniversary, family reunion, honeymoon)
- Interests (adventure, relaxation, food, culture)
- Dietary restrictions or allergies
- Special occasions during stay
- Activity level preferences

- Revenue collected throughout month
- Statement generated by 5th of following month
- Owner payout by 15th of following month
- Automatic ACH deposit

### Guest Financial Operations

**Reservation Payments:**
- Deposit at booking (typically 50%)
- Balance due before arrival
- Automated payment reminders
- Failed payment handling
- Refund processing

**Security Deposits:**
- Collected separately from rental
- Held in trust until checkout
- Damage assessment process
- Refund within defined timeframe
- Claim documentation if withheld

**Tax Collection:**
- Applicable taxes calculated automatically
- TAT, GET, MTA as required
- Collected from guest at booking
- Held in tax reserve
- Remitted on schedule

- Vendor invoice processing

- Approval workflows

- Daily bank reconciliation

- Revenue reconciliation
